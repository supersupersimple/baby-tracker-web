This page will help clear Chrome's PWA cache to fix manifest issues.

Step 1: Clear Service Worker & Cache

Step 2: Manual Chrome Steps

  1. Open Chrome DevTools (F12)
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. In Storage section, click "Clear storage"
  4. Check all boxes and click "Clear site data"
  5. Go to Service Workers and click "Unregister"
  6. Close DevTools and refresh the page

Step 3: Test Again

After clearing, visit: http://localhost:3000

Or test with: PWA Debug Page
